Cat Age Calculator

Find out how old your cat is in human years

This program takes your cats age and turns it into human years by using an incredibly complex algorithm.

Please enter your cats name and age below.








Disclaimer: There is no scientific/official way of translating cat years into human years. This program uses the widely adopted method of adding 4 years, each year, after the age of 2 years. You can read more about this method on Purina's website and the International Cat Care website.
